Axarquia Animal Rescue is run by unpaid volunteers. We rely totally on our supporters for funding and help to carry out the many day to day activities. We would be unable to do what we do without help and support from people like you. Thank you.

Brexit Update

DELIGHTED to say that following a successful animal transport to the UK at the end of January, we are now able to resume adoptions to the UK!
We are of course continuing to work very closely with our trusted transporters to ensure we are aware of any further changes.
